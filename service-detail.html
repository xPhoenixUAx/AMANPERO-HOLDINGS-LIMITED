Summary

Service summary

This service turns a defined business need into a working website, landing page, or web feature. The work can include planning, UX structure, frontend development, backend logic, contact form handling, analytics setup, testing, deployment, and support.

Problems this service solves

  • The current website does not explain services clearly enough.
  • Campaign traffic is being sent to pages that are not built for conversion.
  • Technical ownership is unclear after launch.
  • Forms, analytics, and follow-up paths are disconnected.
  • The company needs a stable base before investing more in advertising.

What is included

  • Requirements review and delivery plan.
  • Sitemap, page structure, and content guidance.
  • Responsive HTML, CSS, and JavaScript implementation.
  • PHP form processing or integration planning.
  • Basic technical SEO structure.
  • Browser and device checks.
  • Launch support and post-launch fixes.
  • Maintenance recommendations.

Our approach

Plan

We clarify audience, conversion goals, content needs, technical constraints, and launch priorities.

Build

We create clean, maintainable pages and connect the required user actions, forms, and tracking.

Improve

We review usage after launch and help prioritize practical improvements based on real signals.

Deliverables

  • Approved page architecture and content structure.
  • Responsive website or landing page codebase.
  • Working contact form path and validation.
  • Launch checklist and handover notes.
  • Support plan for updates, fixes, and improvements.

Best fit clients

This service fits companies launching a new offer, replacing an outdated website, preparing paid campaigns, improving lead quality, or needing a practical technical partner for ongoing website work.

Expected business impact

The expected impact is a clearer customer journey, fewer technical blockers, more reliable inquiry handling, better campaign destinations, and a website that can be maintained after launch.
