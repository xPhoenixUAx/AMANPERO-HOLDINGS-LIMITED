Summary

What this service does

Analytics and support ensure that a website continues to work after launch. The service can include reporting setup, conversion event review, email follow-up systems, content updates, technical fixes, performance checks, maintenance coordination, and a prioritized backlog for ongoing improvement.

Problems this service solves

  • The website is live but nobody owns updates, fixes, or improvements.
  • Analytics are installed but reports do not help the team make decisions.
  • Leads submit forms but follow-up is inconsistent or disconnected.
  • Small issues accumulate until the site feels outdated or unreliable.
  • Campaign learnings are not turned into website improvements.

What is included

  • Analytics and conversion event review.
  • Reporting structure and insight notes.
  • Email follow-up flow planning.
  • Content updates and page adjustments.
  • Bug fixes and technical maintenance coordination.
  • Performance and usability checks.
  • Campaign page updates.
  • Improvement backlog and prioritization.

Our approach

Monitor

We review forms, analytics, page behavior, content needs, and technical issues that affect the website’s usefulness.

Maintain

We handle practical changes: updates, fixes, content edits, campaign page adjustments, and reporting support.

Improve

We turn observations into a prioritized backlog so the next improvement has a reason and a business purpose.

Deliverables

  • Analytics and reporting recommendations.
  • Conversion event and form review notes.
  • Email follow-up system plan where required.
  • Maintenance and update backlog.
  • Monthly or project-based improvement recommendations.

Best fit clients

This service fits companies with an existing website, recently launched projects, teams running ongoing campaigns, or businesses that need a practical partner for updates and improvement rather than a one-time handoff.

Expected business impact

The expected impact is a more reliable website, clearer reporting, faster updates, better follow-up, fewer unresolved technical issues, and a stronger connection between campaigns and website improvements.

FAQ

Can support start after another team built the site?

Yes. Amanpero Agency can review the current setup and define what support is realistic based on the existing codebase and access.

Can this include content updates?

Yes. Content changes, new sections, campaign page updates, and small layout improvements can be included in the support scope.

Is reporting part of maintenance?

It can be. Reporting is most useful when maintenance and improvement work are connected to actual performance signals.
