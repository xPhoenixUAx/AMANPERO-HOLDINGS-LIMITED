Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AMANPERO HOLDINGS LIMITED uses cookies and similar technologies across the website.

Effective date: 1 January 2026
Last updated: 1 January 2026

01. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device, including a computer, tablet, or mobile phone, when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and help website operators understand how their websites are used.

Cookies can be session cookies, which are temporary and deleted when you close your browser, or persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them manually. Cookies can be set either by the website you are visiting (first-party cookies) or by third-party services integrated into that website (third-party cookies).

In addition to cookies, we may also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, local storage objects, and scripts for similar purposes. References to cookies in this policy include all such similar tracking technologies.

This Cookie Policy forms part of our Privacy Policy and should be read together with it.

Under the EU ePrivacy Directive and the GDPR, we are required to obtain your informed consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie consent panel.

02. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the basic functionality of our website, including navigation, security, and contact form submission.
  • To remember your preferences and settings across visits, improving your browsing experience.
  • To analyse how visitors use our website so we can improve its structure, content, and performance.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and understand which channels drive traffic to our website.
  • To support remarketing and retargeting activities, allowing us to show relevant advertisements to previous visitors on third-party platforms such as Google and Meta.
  • To comply with applicable advertising platform requirements, including conversion tracking and campaign performance measurement.

03. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting privacy preferences or submitting contact forms.

You cannot opt out of these cookies because the website cannot function correctly without them.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting aggregated and anonymous usage data.

We use this information to improve website usability, content quality, and performance.

You may opt out of analytics cookies without affecting your ability to use the website.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set by our advertising partners to help deliver relevant advertisements across third-party platforms.

They may be used for audience creation, remarketing, and conversion tracking.

You may opt out of marketing cookies at any time.
