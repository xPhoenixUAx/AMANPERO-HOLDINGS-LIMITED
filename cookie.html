What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by a website. They can support basic website operation, remember preferences, or help measure website and campaign performance.

Essential cookies

Essential cookies may be used to keep the website functioning, protect forms from spam, maintain security, or remember basic consent choices. These are generally required for the website to work properly.

Analytics cookies

If analytics tools are enabled, cookies may help understand visits, page views, traffic sources, device information, and user interactions. This information is used to improve website content and performance.

Marketing cookies

If advertising tools are enabled, marketing cookies may support campaign measurement, remarketing, conversion tracking, or audience insights.

Third-party tools

The website may use third-party tools such as Google Analytics, Google Ads, or Meta Pixel if implemented. These tools may set their own cookies and process data according to their own policies.

Cookie consent

Where required, non-essential cookies should be used only after consent. A future cookie banner or consent platform can be connected to this policy and the website scripts.

Managing preferences

You can manage cookies through your browser settings by deleting existing cookies or blocking future cookies. Some website features may not work correctly if essential cookies are disabled.
