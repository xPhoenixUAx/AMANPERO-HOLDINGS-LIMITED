Summary

What this service does

Performance campaign support focuses on the structure around paid traffic: audience intent, campaign message, creative needs, landing page alignment, conversion events, and reporting. The goal is to avoid isolated ad setup and instead build campaigns that can be reviewed, improved, and connected to business inquiries.

Problems this service solves

  • Paid traffic is being sent to generic pages with weak offer alignment.
  • Campaigns are launched without clear tracking or conversion definitions.
  • Creative, ad copy, and landing pages are not telling the same story.
  • Reports show clicks and spend but do not explain what to improve next.
  • Search and social campaigns are treated as separate efforts instead of a joined acquisition system.

What is included

  • Campaign objective and offer review.
  • Paid social audience and creative direction.
  • Search advertising keyword and intent grouping.
  • Landing page recommendations.
  • Conversion event and tracking plan.
  • Ad message and CTA recommendations.
  • Optimization cadence and reporting notes.
  • Recommendations for tests and next iterations.

Our approach

Map intent

We separate audience segments, search intent, social context, offer awareness, and conversion readiness.

Align assets

We connect ad message, creative direction, landing page content, forms, and tracking before campaigns scale.

Improve loops

We define how performance should be reviewed so the next change is based on evidence rather than guessing.

Deliverables

  • Campaign structure and channel recommendations.
  • Paid social and search message direction.
  • Landing page and creative requirements.
  • Conversion tracking and reporting plan.
  • Optimization backlog for campaign tests.

Best fit clients

This service fits companies preparing to run paid campaigns, relaunching existing campaigns, building dedicated landing pages, or trying to improve the quality of inquiries from advertising activity.

Expected business impact

The expected impact is cleaner campaign logic, stronger relevance between ads and pages, clearer measurement, less wasted spend, and a more useful feedback loop for improving acquisition.

FAQ

Do you manage ad accounts directly?

The scope can focus on campaign planning, landing alignment, creative direction, and optimization support. Account execution can be defined during the proposal stage.

Can this include both Google Ads and social ads?

Yes. The service can cover search advertising, paid social, and the landing pages or tracking required to support both.

Do we need landing pages first?

Not always, but campaigns usually perform better when the destination page is planned around the specific offer and audience.
