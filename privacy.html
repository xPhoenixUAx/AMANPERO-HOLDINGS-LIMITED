Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data AMANPERO HOLDINGS LIMITED collects, how it is used, and what rights you have under applicable data protection laws.

Effective date: 1 January 2026
Last updated: 1 January 2026

01. Who We Are

This website is operated by AMANPERO HOLDINGS LIMITED, a company incorporated in the Republic of Cyprus, with its registered office at:

AMANPERO HOLDINGS LIMITED
Filokyprou, 4, Flat/Office 201

We provide digital marketing, web design, website development, and related business support services to clients across Europe and internationally.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable Cyprus data protection legislation, AMANPERO HOLDINGS LIMITED acts as the data controller in respect of personal data collected through this website.

We are committed to protecting the personal data of our website visitors, prospective clients, and business partners. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

02. Data We Collect

We may collect personal data when you interact with our website, submit a contact form, communicate with us, request a proposal, or engage our services.

The types of data we may collect include:

Identity Data

  • Name
  • Surname
  • Company name
  • Job title

Contact Data

  • Email address
  • Business address
  • Contact details provided through inquiry forms

Financial Data

  • Invoicing details
  • VAT numbers
  • Billing-related information where applicable

Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Time zone settings
  • Operating system and platform

Usage Data

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the website
  • Navigation paths
  • Form interactions
  • Website behavior and engagement

Marketing Data

  • Communication preferences
  • Advertising interaction data
  • Campaign-related engagement

Business Data

  • Project scope
  • Marketing goals
  • Website requirements
  • Service requests
  • Budgets and project timelines where voluntarily provided

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data. If such data is submitted, it will be removed where appropriate.

03. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries and contact requests
  • To provide quotations, proposals, and service-related communication
  • To deliver and improve our services
  • To manage client relationships and project communication
  • To process invoices and business transactions
  • To analyse website performance and user behaviour
  • To support marketing and advertising activities
  • To ensure website security and prevent misuse
  • To comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations

We do not use your personal data for unrelated purposes without informing you.

04. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases for processing personal data:

Contract Performance

When processing is necessary to provide services, prepare proposals, or fulfill contractual obligations.

Legitimate Interests

To operate and improve our business, maintain website security, optimise performance, and manage business relationships.

Legal Obligation

To comply with applicable legal, accounting, tax, and regulatory requirements.

Consent

Where required, such as for marketing communications, cookie preferences, and certain tracking technologies.

You may withdraw your consent at any time where consent is the legal basis for processing.

05. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Typical retention periods include:

  • Client and financial data: up to 7 years for legal and accounting obligations
  • Inquiry and contact form data: up to 2 years
  • Analytics and website usage data: up to 26 months
  • Marketing communication data: until consent is withdrawn or no longer relevant

We may anonymise certain data for analytical and business improvement purposes.

06. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share personal data with trusted third parties where necessary, including:

  • Hosting providers
  • CRM systems
  • Email communication tools
  • Website maintenance providers
  • Advertising platforms such as Google and Meta
  • Analytics providers
  • Legal, accounting, and professional advisers
  • Government authorities or regulators where required by law

All third parties are required to process data securely, lawfully, and only for legitimate business purposes.

07. International Transfers

Some third-party providers, such as Google or Meta, may process personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Approved international data transfer mechanisms
  • GDPR-compliant provider agreements

We take reasonable steps to ensure your data remains protected.

08. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority in Cyprus.

09. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyse website usage
  • Support advertising and remarketing activities
  • Measure campaign effectiveness

For full details about how cookies are used, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
